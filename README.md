Mission: 
This project was started for the Vertically Integrated Projects Program at Stony Brook University. Our team strives to cultivate the community and food distribution efforts on campus. Free food is a strong motivation for students to attend events, so esca is a powerful marketing tool for clubs and organizations. Also, many clubs are forced to throw out leftover food, creating a significant waste problem. By promoting campus events, esca will improve student involvement and reduce food waste around campus.

Features:
- Live, interactive map: Events across campus with free food are highlighted.
- Proximity-based notifications: Optionally, esca will track the user’s location and notify them when they are close to an event.
- Catalog of events: Users can discover events through our catalog and personalize the content they see through robust filtering options.
